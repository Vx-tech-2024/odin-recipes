Tea

Description

Tea is the most common hot beverage taken in most countries in Africa especially Kenya. The above picture represents a cup of kenyan tea cooked by water,milk,tea leaves and sugar as the basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea leaves or any other brewing elements.
  • Sugar; which is optional in most cases.

Steps

  1. Boil your water in a cooking pot.
  2. Add your milk and tea leaves or any other preffered brewing element and allow the mixture to boil.
  3. Sieve your boiled tea into a cup and add preffered sugar using a tablespoon.
  4. Your tea is ready.

Rice

Description

Rice is an african meal mostly preffered due to its easy cooking steps.All it requires is water, rice grains, cooking oil and salt. Please note I am using rice grains to refer to rice before its cooked and rice to refer to cooked one.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice grains
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put water into a container and add your measured rice and wash it.
  2. Soak your rice in clean water.
  3. Measure water into a cooking pot.
  4. Add two spoons of both salt and cooking oil.
  5. Heat until the mixture is hot and not exactly boiled.
  6. Add your soaked rice.
  7. Wait for it to boil for some few minutes.
  8. Reduce the amount of heat supply.
  9. Allow the rice to absorb all the water and heat until it dries and your rice is ready to serve with desired stew.

Ugali

Description

Ugali is a staple food in most parts in Kenya which is made of maize flour and water as the basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Maize flour

Steps

  1. Measure your water and put it into a cooking pot.
  2. Heat until the water boils.
  3. Add little flour and stir and then allow the mixture to boil for a few minutes.
  4. Reduce the amount of heat supply.
  5. Add more flour while stirring until a prefarable texture is achieved.
  6. Allow it to cook for sometime and your ugali is ready to be served with any stew.
